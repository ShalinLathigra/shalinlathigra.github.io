About

Hi! My name is Shalin Lathigra, I'm a 4th year student at Carleton University and amateur Game Developer. I love doing game jams and am currently trying to expand my knowledge of graphical programming. I have experience working with raw C++ and OpenGL, as well as engines such as Unity and Godot. Next on my list to learn is Unreal Engine!

My non-computer related interests include reading (Brandon Sanderson for the win!), bouldering, and playing Dungeons and Dragons. Please feel free to reach out if you'd like to chat!
